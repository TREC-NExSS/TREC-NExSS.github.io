Brianna Loo

Undergraduate Research Aide

  Brianna Loo is an undergraduate student at Arizona State University, studying Environmental Engineering. While her studies focus on Earth’s environment and chemical processes, she has a special interest in the habitability of other planets and is involved in ASU’s NExSS-TREC project. Additionally, she contributes to research on water quality and treatment, as well as solutions for Arctic ice melting, to improve humanity’s relationship with the Earth. Brianna plans to pursue an interdisciplinary approach to her career, engineering solutions to environmental problems.
