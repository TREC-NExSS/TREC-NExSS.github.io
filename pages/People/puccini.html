Gianni Puccini

Undergraduate Research Aide

  Gianni Puccini is a Sophomore at Arizona State University, studying Aerospace Engineering (Astronautics) with a minor in Italian Language/Culture. He is actively involved in ASU’s NExSS TREC project, focusing on the composition of exoplanets and meteoric bodies. Additionally, he is president of ASU's Italian Student Body and plans to pursue a master’s degree in Astronautical Engineering. Gianni hopes to find a career that combines his Astronautical Engineering major, Astrophysical research, and Italian studies.
